Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic

The Dominican Republic is a Caribbean nation located on the island of Hispaniola, which it shares with Haiti. Known for its rich culture, the country boasts a vibrant history shaped by indigenous, African, and European influences. The Dominican Republic is famous for its stunning beaches, lush mountains, and warm tropical climate. Its capital, Santo Domingo, is the oldest continuously inhabited city in the Americas and holds UNESCO World Heritage status. The country is also renowned for its music, such as merengue and bachata, and is a popular destination for tourists seeking both relaxation and adventure.
