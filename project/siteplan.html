Dominican Republic Showcase - Project Site Plan

Site Name

Dominican Republic Showcase

This name reflects the website’s purpose: to present the beauty, culture, and uniqueness of the Dominican Republic in an engaging and interactive way. It effectively communicates the site's focus and makes it easy for visitors to understand what to expect.

Optional domain availability: dominicanshowcase.com

Site Purpose

The Dominican Republic Showcase website is designed to highlight the country's culture, traditions, food, and landmarks in an interactive way. The site will serve as both an educational resource and a fun exploration of what makes the Dominican Republic unique.

Visitors will be able to:

  • Learn about Dominican traditions, festivals, and music.
  • Discover iconic landmarks and must-try Dominican dishes.
  • Engage with interactive features like a random proverb generator and a random recipe generator.
  • Enjoy fun facts and trivia about the country’s history, famous figures, and achievements.

This project also serves as a way for me to showcase my web development skills, focusing on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while sharing something deeply personal and meaningful.

Scenarios

  1. Travel Inquiry: "I’m planning a trip to the Dominican Republic. What are the must-visit landmarks?"
  2. Cooking Exploration: "I want to cook a traditional Dominican meal. What’s a good recipe to start with?"
  3. Cultural Inquiry: "What are some popular festivals in the Dominican Republic, and when do they take place?"

Color Schema

  • Dominican Blue (#002D62): Used for headers, navigation, and text accents.
  • Dominican Red (#CE1126): Used for buttons, highlights, and active elements.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Used for backgrounds and to provide contrast with text.
  • Warm Yellow (#F4A900): Used for interactive features and additional accents.

Typography

  • Motserrat (Sans-serif): Used for all main headings.
  • Lora (Serif): Used for body text and descriptions.
  • Pacifico (Cursive): Used for special elements such as quotes and interactive features.
